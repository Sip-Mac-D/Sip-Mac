## Overview

Sip Professional Suite is a complete color management platform that serves design teams with the palette sharing, contrast checking, and integration depth needed for consistent color usage across professional design and development workflows. It extends individual color picking into team-wide color management with features that enforce brand consistency and accessibility standards.

Contrast ratio checking evaluates text and background color combinations against WCAG accessibility guidelines, identifying combinations that fail to meet minimum contrast requirements for readable text. The contrast checker provides pass/fail results for AA and AAA compliance levels, enabling designers to verify accessibility during the design process rather than discovering violations during review. Suggested adjustments propose modified colors that meet contrast requirements while staying close to the original selection.

Team palette sharing distributes color palettes across team members with synchronized updates that ensure everyone works with current brand colors. When the design system's primary color changes, the update propagates to every team member's palette automatically. Shared palettes include usage notes and context that help team members apply colors correctly without referring to external documentation.

Design tool integration syncs colors between Sip and Sketch, Figma, Adobe XD, and other design tools, maintaining consistency between the color picker and the design environment. Colors picked in Sip appear in design tool palettes, and colors defined in design tools appear in Sip. This bidirectional sync ensures that the working palette is always current regardless of which tool is active.

Sip Professional Suite is the color management platform for design teams who need shared, accessible, and consistently formatted color management that bridges the gap between color selection and color implementation across design and development workflows. Its team features transform color from an individual creative decision into a managed organizational asset.

## Performance & Integration

Sip is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Sip processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
